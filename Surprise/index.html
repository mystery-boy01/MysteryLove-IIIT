INTRODUCTION

Hello everyone, I know whoever is reading this must be wondering what this is, who is this for, and who wrote it. For now, let these questions remain unanswered. This has been created to carry my writings to her. There is a small story behind all of this. I will tell you little part of my story but before that I want to say one thing — don’t try to guess who I am. For now, let me remain a mystery. So, let’s begin...

There is someone in college who has quietly become special to me. Who she is, and how she became special… that’s a long story — one I might tell someday. Usually, someone becomes special when you’ve spent time with them, talked to them, shared moments… but my story isn’t like that. When I first came to college, she was just another face in the crowd for me. But one day, something in my life changed completely. If I go back two years, there was a time when I used to write Shayari for someone… but that had stopped. Since 2024, I hadn’t written a single line for anyone. Then one evening, I saw her. She looked unbelievably beautiful — wearing a simple kurti, earrings gently swaying, a small bindi on her forehead, dimples on her cheeks, strands of hair falling forward… and the way she softly adjusted them. And that moment… changed everything. Since then, I began noticing her every day. And with every little observation, I found myself writing again. It was after 2024 that I started writing once more… and somewhere in that process, she became special to me.

And the truth is every word I write now is only for her. I don’t really know what this feeling is yet, maybe it’s just admiration or maybe something deeper, but one thing I know for sure is that she became someone who brought my words back, and every line I write now somehow finds its way to her.

— Me, The Mystery
